About Me

In my free time, I enjoy listening to music, especially pop-punk and rock. In addition to that, I love to craft! I love making things, whether it is painting, drawing, wreath-making, jewelry-making, scrapbooking, or crocheting.

When I'm not making something, I'm usually trying to get through the many piles of unread books in my house. I love to read and would have to say my favorite genres are fantasy and young adult fiction. Some of my recent favorites are: The Unspoken Name by A. K. Larkwood, Ninth House by Leigh Bardugo, The Beautiful Ones by Silvia Moreno-Garcia, and Six of Crows by Leigh Bardugo.

Have any song or book suggestions? Let me know below!
